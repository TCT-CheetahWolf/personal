TCT, or also known as Cheetah Wolf, is a content creator.


Hailing from the world's unknown, he continually learn to make the most of his abilities, in order to create more and efficiently. However, as it seems, he doesn't perform well in designing.


Official Introduction

A wandering wolf who has but the ability to read visuals and graphics. As his talents progress, he too continues to strive for a living.


Cheetah Wolf dwells detached from the real world. His physical self may remains, however his true soul has never really coexisted with him. With that implied, he has poor grasp of the social interaction, appearing as an eccentric and anomalous being.


As a knowledge seeker, he rises up day by day to become a worthy entity as well as a worthy designer. But would he desire to free himself from the cage, as if the haste of the world wouldn't be chasing him?


In return, with his patience and resilience throughout time, he gradually came back to life and made companions in his wandering days. He put his most of efforts and yearnings to maintain the bond, however life would strike him once more and all returns to silence, as if he had existed before.


All would he only see is a fractured dream in a dream...


Whether or not will he become a caged bird for an eternity? Likewise, keep wandering with eons of sufferings?

Lore

TCT originally started with the mascot "Cheetah Teddy" of the game Dancing Line. As he matures, the mascot evolves into Cheetah Fox, and finally turning to a final form, Cheetah Wolf. Their images are shown as a gallery below.
